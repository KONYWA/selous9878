About Us

Welcome to Mafoy Africa Safaris, where we bring the best of Tanzania to the world! Since our founding in 2024, we have been dedicated to providing our clients with personalized and unforgettable safari experiences.

From wildlife adventures in Serengeti to cultural immersion in Maasai villages, we are committed to offering you the most diverse and exciting tours in Tanzania.

What We Offer

Wildlife Safaris

Explore Tanzania's world-famous parks, including Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater, with expert guides.

Mountain Climbing

Reach the summit of Mount Kilimanjaro and other incredible peaks with our experienced trekking guides.

Cultural Tours

Experience the traditions and customs of Tanzania's indigenous tribes with authentic cultural experiences.

Beach Holidays

Relax on the pristine beaches of Zanzibar, with crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life.

Dream Vacation

Book Your Dream Vacation Now!

A Tanzania vacation is the ultimate once-in-a-lifetime holiday! Tanzania is a top safari destination with breathtaking landscapes, vibrant wildlife, and an unforgettable cultural experience. Whether you dream of exploring the endless plains of the Serengeti, witnessing the Great Migration, or marveling at the Ngorongoro Crater, Tanzania offers something for everyone.

You can unwind on the pristine beaches of Zanzibar Island, known for its turquoise waters and white sands, or challenge yourself with an exhilarating climb up Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak. The possibilities are endless, and every moment promises memories to last a lifetime.
